Thinking and Language

I am interested in the role of language in thinking; this is surprisingly hard to pin down and I believe asking people about what they feel is going on in their own heads might be useful. I am intending to have a series of structured conversations with people to see what we learn that way. This website will present the transcripts of those conversations. The proceedure for posting these transcripts includes a period when participants have the opportunity to withdraw their interview and so it will be some time before any transcripts actually appear.
