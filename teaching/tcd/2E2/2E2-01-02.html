Exam

The exam format will be very similar to last years with two questions on Laplace transforms, two on Z transforms, two on differencial equations, one of which will involve linearizations, one question on series solutions and two on vector calculus. The questions are very similar to the problem sheets, it would be very useful to try questions from the problem sheets and past papers without looking at the solutions.
